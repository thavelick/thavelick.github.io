Comments:

Due to its 28 page length Egmont decided to split this story into 4 parts of almost equal size in their Scandinavian editions. Since Don Rosa has only done a one-part version of this story (Gladstone didn't need anything else) - Egmont had to remove some panels to give place to the summary in the beginning of parts 2 and 3. In part2 they even removed the left part of another panel and thus erased a lady with a pig's nose. In 2001 enclosures with the one-part version of this story saw print in Norway and Denmark, but not in Sweden.
