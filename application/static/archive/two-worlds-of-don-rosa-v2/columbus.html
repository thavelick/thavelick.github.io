Comments:

Don Rosa has made both a 3- and a 1-part version for this. The extra art for the 3-part-version wasn't published anywhere until it was published in the American Barks/Rosa Collection #3 in 2008. Sweden would have been the place, but they didn't see the story until 2008, and by then it was the one-parter that was published. Don Rosa told me that the 1-part version is different from the 3-parter by having a few more panels on page 9, and by not having a summary-box on page 17. Apart from that summary-box page 17 and the third part's splash are alike, showing the Library of Alexandria.
