Comments:

This story was first published as a 2-parter in Scandinavia in May 2002. Don Rosa also made a 1-part version of this story, but that version doesn't seem to have been published anywhere before it was finally published by Gemstone in December 2003. France would have been the place, but with editor Pascal Pierrey burdened with other tasks, the redaction of the Picsou Magazine for some reason managed to use the serialized version even though the story was printed in one part.
