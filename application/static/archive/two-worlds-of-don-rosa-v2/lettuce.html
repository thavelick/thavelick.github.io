Comments:

Once Don Rosa reconstructed two panels for the old Barks-classic "How green was my lettuce", printed in the American Uncle $crooge magazine no 241 (published in February 1990). The reason was that this story was cut when it was first published back in 1964. When Gladstone eventually decided to print the whole story, the publisher found all but two panels, so they asked Don Rosa to redraw them.
