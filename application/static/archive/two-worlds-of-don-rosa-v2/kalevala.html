Comments:

This story is with it's 33 pages Don Rosa's longest story, and for the first time ever the parts weren't the same length. The first part is 12 pages and following the 13th page is the splash in the 3-part version, and a 5-panel-half-page in the 1-part. These 5 panels have only been published in Finland and Germany as far as I know, and I got help from Janne Heino to translate the dialogue of that half-page, and page 23 and 34. Page 23 looks very much alike in the two versions. Exceptions are that the 1-part-version's panel is a bit lifted up and there's an extra bubble of dialogue. The 34th page is only published in Finland.
