Comments:

Due to its 28 page length Egmont decided to split this story into 4 parts of equal size in their Scandinavian editions. Since Don Rosa has only done a one-part version of this story (Gladstone didn't need anything else) - Egmont had to remove some panels to give place to the summary in the beginning of parts 3 and 4. However to reduce the lack of information to a minimum, panels other than those in the upper left corner were removed and the rest was slightly rearranged. Thus panel 4 on page 15 and panel 4 on page 22 are the missing panels in the Scandinavian editions. Since it's now more than 10 years ago since last time this story was published in Scandinavia, there is good hope that the Scandinavian readers will se a complete reprint of this story in a not to distant future.
