Comments:

For some reason Egmont decided to split this story into 2 parts in their Scandinavian editions, even though it's only 12 pages long. Since there are no 1/2-page splash panel anywhere in the story but on page 1, they had to remove a panel to give place to the summary in the beginning of part 2. Since it's now more than 10 years ago since last time this story was published in Scandinavia, there is good hope that the Scandinavian readers will se a complete reprint of this story in a not to distant future.
