My Web Archive

There are several websites I created that have died and been saved by archive.org or oocitties.org. I've recovered them here.

Chaos of the Mind

This was one of my first websites, created around 1996 and maintained until sometime in 1998. Some interesting pages:

  • The Main Page
  • Imperial Software - A page for a game I created around this time
  • The Classic RPG database - A list of rpg games for 80s and 90s game systems. I rebuilt this as a proper database years later, but this one is arguably cooler
  • How to Make Ukrainian Easter Eggs - I built this little site instructing on how to make Ukrainian Easter Eggs as part of Russian class in high school. Sadly the Ingredients page is missing due to a linking error, but who knows maybe someone will find it useful.

The Two Worlds of Don Rosa

When I was heavily collecting Uncle Scrooge comics around 2000, I worked with Anders Christian Sivebaek to create a site to explore the difference between stories as published in the U.S. and as published in Europe.

After I lost interest (and the free hosting from my university) in Uncle Scrooge collecting, Anders and Sigvald Grøsfjeld jr. built a new site that was much more comprehensive. It actually featured every story with differences between the US and European versions. Around 2013 their site went offline, so I'm also archiving it here.
